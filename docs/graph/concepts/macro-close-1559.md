# Macro close (15:59 candle)

The 15:59:00–15:59:59 ET closing-auction candle of the [[macro-window]] (bucket `k359`,
normalised to relative 5-second buckets 0..11) — where the late close-flow reversal effect
concentrates (final 15/10 seconds). A **variant** of the macro window shared by multiple studies.
