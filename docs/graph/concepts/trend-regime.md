# Trend / regime

The classification of market state — trending vs ranging/mean-reverting, and its strength —
that provides context for macro behaviour. Built from a battery of indicators ([[adx]],
[[atr-range-ratio]], [[dra]], [[irr]], [[lag-autocorr-hurst]], [[mss]], [[swing-point-density]],
[[efficiency-ratio]], [[variance-ratio]]) combined by a [[state-detector]] and tracked over
history by [[historical-regimes]]. Modeled toward a [[trendability]] target.
