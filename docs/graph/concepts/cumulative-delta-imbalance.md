# Cumulative delta imbalance

Accumulated [[volume-delta]] left **unresolved** before a decision point, used as a predictor
of subsequent flow. Variants seen in the macro studies:

- `eth_only_pre350`, `rth_only_pre350`, `eth_rth_pre350` — accumulated through 15:49 ET.
- `eth_rth_pre59`, `eth_rth_macro_pre59`, `rth_macro_pre59` — through the 15:59 close approach.

Empirically tends to precede **opposite-signed** impulse/close flow (a reversal tilt), cleanest
when the accumulated imbalance is strongly positive.
